Our Story

Autumn Bakery began not in a professional kitchen, but in a quiet home corner fueled by simple curiosity. What started as a personal quest to master the perfect velvety cheesecake quickly blossomed into an obsession with the alchemy of baking.

From those early experiments, my love for the craft expanded—from perfecting the crumb of a signature cookie to mastering the delicate, salty bite of freshly baked sourdough bread. Every recipe here is a testament to those slow Sunday afternoons spent refining, tasting, and sharing the warmth of a home-baked treat.
